Activities

Boating

Cossack is a coastal town, with many opportunities for boating, offering ocean access from the local estuaries.

Swimming

Cossack boasts almost unlimited beachside access along its coast!

Fishing

Visit Cossack's dedicated fishing pier, just along Cossack Road! At 6 meters deep, and with a balmy ocean view, you are sure to put dinner on the table!

Indigenous Awareness

Cossack is a small but significant part of Indigenous history. Come and see our landmarks!

Dining

Come join us for a bite at the Blue SPrings Hotels Bar and Grill. We serve the freshest (locally caught!) seafood, local produce and ice cold drinks!
